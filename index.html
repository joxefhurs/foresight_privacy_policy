Foresight Financials Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 4, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Foresight Financials ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

We take the privacy of your information very seriously. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring its confidentiality. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have about your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information through the App:

  • Personal Information: This includes information that can be used to identify you directly, such as:
    • Email Address
    • Password (hashed and securely stored)
    • Phone Number (optional)
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes information that does not identify you directly, such as:
    • Device information (type, model, operating system)
    • Usage data (app features used, frequency of use)

We may collect additional information with your consent, such as your financial goals or investment preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and ensure secure access to the App.
  • To verify your phone number (if provided) for two-factor authentication.
  • To manage your user account and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience within the App by tailoring content and features based on your usage patterns (with your consent).
  • To improve the App by analyzing usage data and identifying areas for improvement.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encrypting your data in transit and at rest.
  • Implementing industry-standard security protocols.
  • Regularly reviewing and updating our security measures.

5. Account Deletion

You have the option to delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all of your personal information will be removed from our systems. However, some non-personal information may be retained in an anonymized format for analytical purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App, such as data storage providers or security vendors. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and secure. We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal requirements, protect the safety of others, or investigate potential fraud.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or comply with legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on the App. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of any updates.
